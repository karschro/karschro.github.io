I'm a research scientist and engineer in applied AI, brain-computer interfaces, and computational neuroscience. I currently manage research operations at Basis Research Institute, where I work on meta-science for AI research.

Previously, I was a postdoc in Mark Churchland's lab at Columbia's Zuckerman Institute, where we combined novel machine learning techniques with electrophysiology to demonstrate real-time BCI control algorithms in primates. Before that, I was a graduate student in Cindy Chestek's neuroprosthetics lab at Michigan.

See my papers and patents on Google Scholar.
